Features

Powerful tools to manage every aspect of your school

Attendance Tracking

Mark daily attendance digitally. Generate monthly reports and notify parents of absences automatically.

Fee Management

Track fee payments, send reminders, generate receipts, and manage scholarships and discounts.

Exam Management

Create exam schedules, enter marks, calculate grades, and print report cards with one click.

Class Timetable

Build and manage class schedules. Avoid conflicts and share timetables with teachers and students.

Library Management

Track books, manage issue and return, and send overdue reminders to students.

Admin Dashboard

Get a complete overview of school operations with charts, stats, and real-time notifications.

Parent Portal

Parents can log in to view their child's attendance, grades, fee status, and school announcements — keeping families connected and informed.

  • View attendance and grades online
  • Pay fees securely through the portal
  • Receive SMS and email notifications
  • Download report cards and certificates
